When we feel like frauds, our thoughts are cluttered. We are overwhelmed by a chaotic mix of tasks, criticisms, and expectations. Graphic design teaches us how to solve this using **negative space, hierarchy, and alignment**.

Applying design principles to your mental space works wonders:



- **Negative Space (White Space):** Give yourself breathing room. Say no to non-essential commitments to allow your mind space to focus and rest.

- **Hierarchy:** Not all feedback is created equal. Put constructive critiques from trusted mentors at the top, and filter anonymous or malicious criticisms to the absolute bottom.

- **Alignment:** Align your daily actions with your core values. When your work matches your personal truth, self-doubt loses its grip.



A clean canvas creates a clean mind. Design your lifestyle with the same intent and minimalism you apply to your screens.
