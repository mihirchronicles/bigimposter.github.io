title: "Dance Your Way Out"
description: "A short poem on dancing to get out of your head and into your body."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Turn the music up too loud\
and let your body answer first.\
No one's watching in this crowd -\
it is just you, and a steady burst.

The mind that judges every step\
forgets itself when hips take lead.\
You are not performing, you are not kept\
to counts of four, to forms, to creed.

Let your arms go where they want,\
let your knees forget the rules.\
This is not a test, a pompous brag -\
just a body, loosening its limbs.

When the song winds down to quiet,\
notice what has come undone.\
You danced your way out of the riot\
of thoughts - and for now, you've won.
