title: "Pick It Up Just to Play"
description: "A short poem on playing an instrument or game with no goal but joy."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Pick up the instrument, the controller,\
the deck of cards, the worn-out ball.\
Not to get better, not to grow taller\
in skill — just to play, that's all.

Remember when this was the point,\
before the lessons, before the score,\
before you felt the need to anoint\
every hour with something more.

Play badly. Play just for the feel\
of fingers, hands, or feet in motion.\
There's nothing here that's at stake or real\
except a small, returning notion:

that joy doesn't need a reason,\
that play was never meant to prove.\
Let this be an offseason\
from everything you have to improve.
