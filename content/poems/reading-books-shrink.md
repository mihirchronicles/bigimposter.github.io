title: "Other Voices, Same Fear"
description: "A short poem on reading other writers to feel less alone in self-doubt."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Open a page some stranger wrote\
in some quiet, doubting year.\
Find the worry that they noted,\
find that you are not the only here.

A poet too once felt this small,\
once stared at blank and empty light.\
Their words still stand against the wall\
of every long, uncertain night.

Borrow their courage for a while,\
let their rhythm steady yours.\
Let their honesty, their style,\
open up some inner doors.

You are not alone in this.\
The shelf is full of those who felt\
exactly what you can't dismiss —\
and still they wrote, and still they dealt.
