title: "Write to No One"
description: "A short poem on journaling as a private place to set down the day's noise."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Open the notebook, write the date,\
then write whatever's in your head.\
No structure here, no need to wait\
for something wise or well-bred.

Write about the meeting that went sideways,\
the thing you wish you'd said instead.\
Write about the long, strange highways\
your thoughts took before you went to bed.

No one will grade this. No one's reading.\
This page exists for only you.\
It's not output, it's not pleading,\
it's just a place to work things through.

Close the notebook. Feel the weight\
of what you carried, now set down.\
Tomorrow there's a fresh blank slate,\
and today's noise won't follow you around.
