title: "Loosen the Knot"
description: "A short poem on walking away from the desk to let a tight mind unclench."

---

When the page won't move, and your\
shoulders climb up toward your ears,\
stand up, leave the desk, and walk —\
the answer isn't always there.

Your feet know something thinking doesn't,\
a rhythm older than your doubt.\
Step by step, the knot loosens,\
the noise begins to thin out.

You don't have to solve it now,\
or prove that you were right to pause.\
Just walk until you notice how\
the tightness eases, without cause.

Come back to the page renewed,\
not because you forced a fix,\
but because you simply moved —\
and sometimes that's the only trick.
