title: "Say It Out Loud to Someone Else"
description: "A short poem on teaching what you know as proof that you actually know it."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Find the thing you think you're faking,\
the skill you're sure is mostly luck,\
and explain it — slow, unshaking —\
to someone who's just starting up.

Watch the words come out in order,\
watch the pieces start to fit.\
What felt like standing on a border\
turns out to be a thing you've split

into steps that someone else can follow.\
That's not luck. That's not chance.\
That's the kind of thing that's hollow\
only if you never glance

back at how much you actually carry.\
Teaching shows you what you know.\
The fraud who whispered you should worry\
just watched you help someone else grow.
