title: "Slow Enough to Trust"
description: "A short poem on gardening as proof that growth doesn't need to be seen to be real."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Plant the seed and walk away.\
It won't grow faster watched, or rushed.\
There's no metric for today,\
no deadline that can be crushed.

The roots are working underground,\
unseen, unmeasured, out of sight.\
Competence grows the same way — sound,\
slow, and steady, out of sight.

You won't see results this week,\
maybe not even this whole season.\
But tend it anyway — don't seek\
proof before you have a reason.

One day there's a bud, a shoot,\
small evidence of all that's grown.\
You didn't force it. You took root.\
And patience was a skill you'd shown.
