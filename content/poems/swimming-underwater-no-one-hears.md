title: "Underwater, No One Hears"
description: "A short poem on swimming laps to leave the noise of the day above the surface."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Slip into the water slow,\
feel the world above go quiet.\
Down here, there's nowhere left to go\
but forward, through the blue and white.

Your thoughts can't follow past the surface,\
they wait up there, dry and loud.\
Down here it's just breath and purpose,\
stroke by stroke, uncrowned.

No one critiques a length of pool.\
No one's watching for mistakes.\
The water doesn't call you a fool,\
it just holds you while it takes

you forward, lap by steady lap,\
until the noise upstairs is gone.\
Climb out, towel off, close the gap\
between the panic and the calm.
