title: "Sing Until It Settles"
description: "A short poem on humming or singing to calm a racing, doubting mind."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

When the panic tightens in,\
don't think your way back out.\
Open up and let begin\
a low and steady sound.

Hum a note, hold it long,\
feel it move through chest and jaw.\
This isn't about being strong,\
it's about following a law

older than your worried mind:\
breath and rhythm, in and out,\
calm the body, ease the bind,\
quiet down the inner shout.

Sing badly, sing off-key,\
no one's grading what you do.\
Let the sound move through and free\
the place where panic used to live in you.
