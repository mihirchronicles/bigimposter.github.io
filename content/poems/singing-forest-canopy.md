title: "A Song Without Walls"
description: "A short poem on singing outdoors, away from rooms that measure every note."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Step outside the grid of walls,\
the rooms that measure every sound.\
Find a place where no one calls\
your singing wrong, or out of bound.

Under trees, the air is wide.\
No reviews, no scoring sheet.\
A leaf does not stand judged with pride\
or fear that it's not quite complete.

Let your voice go where it wants,\
loud or soft, off-key, unplanned.\
The forest doesn't keep accounts\
of every note, or where you stand.

Come back in, carrying that ease,\
the feeling that you, too, can grow\
without the constant need to please —\
a song that no one scored, but you let go.
