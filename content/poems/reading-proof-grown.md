title: "Proof You've Grown"
description: "A short poem on rereading your own old work to see how far you've come."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Go back to something old you wrote,\
the early lines, the clumsy start.\
Read it now, and take note\
of the distance, and the art.

You winced back then at every word,\
sure that it gave you away.\
Now it reads like something heard\
from someone finding their own way.

The gap between then and now\
is not luck, it's not pretend.\
It's the slow and steady how\
of someone who did not stop or bend.

So when today feels just as raw,\
remember what this reading shows —\
the page that scared you, flaw by flaw,\
is proof of how a writer grows.
