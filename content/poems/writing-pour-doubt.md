title: "Pour the Doubt Onto the Page"
description: "A short poem on writing down the inner critic until it loses its size."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

The voice that says you don't belong,\
that says the others know much more —\
write it down, make it a song,\
a verse to drag onto the shore.

It feels enormous in your head,\
a shadow with no shape or name.\
But once it's lying there instead,\
in ink, it's smaller than your shame.

Now write beneath it, line by line,\
one thing you know is true and yours.\
Let your own words start to shine\
brighter than the closing doors.

Read it back. The voice grows thin,\
it cannot hold the weight it had.\
You wrote your way to letting in\
the truth that you were never bad.
