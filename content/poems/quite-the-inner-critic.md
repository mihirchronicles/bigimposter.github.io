title: "Quite The Inner Critic"
description: "A short poem on writing for no one, just to quiet the inner critic."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Take the page no one will read,\
the one that asks for nothing back.\
Let the messy first thoughts bleed -\
without attack, without violence.

The critic waits outside the door,\
who cannot enter locked-up rooms.\
Write the lines you are hiding for,\
and watch how quickly fear consumes itself.

When given nothing real\
to argue with, to cut or to grade\
it turns its back\
away from the door.

Fold the page, tuck it away.\
It did its work, it set you free.\
Tomorrow brings another day\
to write the things you need to see.
