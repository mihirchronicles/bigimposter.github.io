title: "Color Over the Noise"
description: "A short poem on painting over harsh words until only the true ones remain."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Take the page of harsh remarks,\
the email, the review, the list.\
Cover it in heavy marks\
until the noise no longer exists.

Leave a few words standing free,\
small and quiet, clear and true.\
Let them form a poem to be\
the only thing that's left for you.

What you choose to leave unhidden\
is the story you now tell.\
The rest stays buried, dark, forbidden,\
you decide what rings the bell.

A blank page once held you small.\
Now it's yours to fill with light.\
Paint over doubt and watch it fall\
into the quiet, settled night.
