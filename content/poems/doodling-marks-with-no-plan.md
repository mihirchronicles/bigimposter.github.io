title: "Marks With No Plan"
description: "A short poem on doodling without a project, a client, or a judge."
readTime: "2 min read"
---

Take a pen, take any paper,\
and make a mark with no plan in mind.\
Not a sketch, not a project, not a caper —\
just a line, then another, intertwined.

Let it loop, let it tangle,\
let it wander off the page's edge.\
There's no angle here to wrangle,\
no client, no judge, no pledge.

If it's ugly, that's allowed.\
If it's nothing, that's allowed too.\
This is just your hand, unbowed,\
moving because it wanted to.

Look down at what you've made,\
a mess of lines, and that's just fine.\
Some part of you that felt afraid\
just spent ten minutes feeling fine.
