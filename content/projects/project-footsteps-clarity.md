**Haikus of the Forest** is a digital photo essay and shader project that explores mindfulness. A series of natural forest landscapes are rendered through a custom WebGL fragment shader.

Initially, the images are highly pixelated and blurred, representing the overwhelming fog of anxiety and self-doubt. As the viewer scrolls, the custom code shader gradually resolves the image into a sharp, focused landscape of trees. Alongside the visual resolution, short, nature-inspired haikus slowly fade onto the canvas, mirroring the clarity and peace of mind that comes from stepping away from screens and finding poetic focus in nature.
