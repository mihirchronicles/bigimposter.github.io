**The Poetry Shield** is a visual art series of hand-pulled screenprints and expressive charcoal overlaying on heavy raw canvas. These pieces display stylized book spines stacked to form defensive shields, symbolizing the cognitive barrier that literature and poetry provide.

Adorning the margins are hand-written stanzas of therapeutic poems. Cruel, critical phrases like 'fraud' and 'cheat' are depicted as arrows and spears that physically bounce off the screenprinted book canopies. The dark charcoal captures the heavy emotional texture of self-doubt, while the colored inks of the book titles represent the protective light of self-acceptance.
