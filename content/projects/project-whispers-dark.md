**Whispers in the Dark** is a sensory audio installation that’ll take your senses on a journey. Imagine stepping into a completely dark space, except for three bright spotlights illuminating the floor.

As you walk through the unlit areas, directional dome speakers start playing soft, overlapping whispers of self-doubt. You might hear things like, ‘You’re not good enough,’ or ‘They’ll find out.’ But here’s the twist: if you step directly under one of the spotlights, the whispers disappear, replaced by clear voice recordings of people sharing their stories of overcoming obstacles and finding self-worth.

This project explores the fascinating transition from feeling isolated and anxious in the shadows to experiencing a sense of illumination and shared understanding. It’s a powerful reminder that we’re all in this together, and that our struggles can be overcome.
