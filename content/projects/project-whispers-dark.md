**Echoes of the Lyric** is a sensory audio installation exploring isolation and shared understanding. The gallery space is kept entirely dark, save for three circular pools of light cast by overhead spotlights onto the floor.

In the dark zones, directional dome speakers play a low, overlapping soundscape of whispers representing imposter doubts ("You don't belong here," "You're going to fail"). However, once a visitor steps directly into one of the bright spotlights, the whispering audio cuts out. In its place, the speaker plays clear, intimate recordings of spoken-word poetry about self-acceptance and creative resilience. The transition mimics the shift from isolated, dark overthinking to the light of creative expression and shared truth.
